Exciting Title

Previous Next

  • Play Now

The golden rule of a platformer is keep going right. The further right you go, the closer you are to victory. Exciting Title plays with this idea, by giving the player a choice of left or right. Follow the status quo and brute force your way to the right with the Brawn challenges that will test your platforming skill. Or defy expectations by heading left to take on the Brain challenges in a test of puzzle prowess. Complete both sides to take on the ultimate test of Brain and Brawn.

A project from HackUCI 2018.
