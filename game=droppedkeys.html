Oops, I Dropped My Keys

It doesn't get worse than losing your keys... Well, maybe its's worse when you're stranded in space, and those keys are the only thing that can get you home! In Oops, I Dropped My Keys, your space shuttle has lost power. Luckily, this ship was designed with an emergency backup generator. If only you could remember where you placed the key cards to access it. This virtual reality game places you in the cockpit as you solve puzzles to collect keys. Press buttons and flip switches to get closer to your goal, or just play around with the easter egg, zero gravity wall. Insert all three access keys and you're ready to set a course for planet Earth!

A project from Global Game Jam 2021, where the theme was "Lost & Found"
